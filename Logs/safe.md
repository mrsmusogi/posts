I have been thinking about the present struggle of feminism, which is still ongoing despite being masked by over-representation. The task of contemporary feminism can't be read in the likes of Hidden Figures or the girly ghost busters, tales where oppression is relegated to an ephemeral backdrop that can be worked within should the lady have the requisite chops needed to dually ignore and persist.

For a long time, I have been thinking about how I might extend the screenings & discussions I curate and facilitate into the digital space. and I am driven partly by nostalgia for this internet era. I am also, however, driven by a sincere interest in expanding the confines of the internet. It's absolutely astounding to have these tools to communicate across such distance, and getting sucked into a doomscroll on some tech monopoly's janky ad-infested platform is just a bleak limitation of this possibility. 

I was very online circa 2009 - 2016, when social media actually facilitated social interactions. I am deeply nostalgic for this time. I had no active social media accounts between 2017 and 2023, but I went back online after I moved to Warsaw during a productive quarter-life crisis. I was convinced around that I needed social media to stay in the loop with my friends and support my freelance work by being vaguely omnipresent and available to follow on request. Since 2023, I've learned that meaningful interactions require a lot of work, which social media flattens into something smooth, easy and superficial. 

Now, I'm eager to get off social media slowly and intentionally, rather than just disappearing like I did in 2017. My first attempt at doing this has been facilitated by this website, but I've been thinking about creating a mailing list for a long time (my favourite forms of distanced communication are phone calls and email). So this is me, trying that. 

mrsmusogi mails will be a monthly email, in which I'll attempt to start a conversation. It's participatory, so I encourage you to respond with...

mrsmusogi mails will contain one or all of the following:

short text reflecting on books and articles I've read, or films I've seen. Guiding questions will always figure as part of this writing exercise. texts will reflect on form and probably trail off into tangentially related thoughts on themes present

a link to a short film, some thoughts and some guiding questions (digital version of the screenings and discussions facilitate IRL)

updates from me - events I'm doing or events I am supporting by sharing news of them with you, comic book updates and excerpts and some drawings maybe

weather where i am / have been - i love the weather and i'm very itinerant, i've been collecting fun TUIs, and online ephemera about the weather it will be fun to share






This is my attempt to put some work into my digital interactions. It is an experiment, which means it might fail, but an experiment is something that can be learned from either way. 

I am starting a participatory mailing list. It will feature some thoughts about texts I've read, films I've seen, or experiences I've had. It may feature some short films and guiding questions to consider. I will always have questions to offer. Alongside the main post will be some updates from me. Essentially, it will begin by being a means for me to log off social media and still stay in touch more meaningfully online.

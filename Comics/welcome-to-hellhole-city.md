Welcome to Hellhole City (2026) is a comic book about neurotic cleanliness, recycling as religious experience, and the practice of digging and filling holes to cope with the universal void.

I worked on this comic during a period of interest in psychoanalysis and while I was on the Newbridge Project's Collective Studio artist development programme.

You can purchase a copy from the following independent zine distributors:
- [Antipode Zines](https://antipodezines.com/), Norwich
- [Good Press](https://goodpress.co.uk/), Glasgow

You can have a read of the comic for free at the following zine libraries:
- [BALTIC Zine Library](https://archive.baltic.art/object/miranda-mungai-welcome-to-hellhole-city-ma3607), Gateshead

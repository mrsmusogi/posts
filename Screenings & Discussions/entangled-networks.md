I was selected from an open call to deliver three screening & discussion events responding to the theme of "Reclaiming Digital Agency" for IMPAKT Festival 2023, Our Terms, Our Conditions. I curated one short film per event, researched the themes, invited participants from Policy Officers to digital artists and facilitated each open discussion.

**ENTANGLED NETWORKS** is a trio of events that considers the complicated, intertwined nature of data and algorithms. The events are an invitation to think through dialogue, untangling how we might reclaim digital agency in favour of community, and against categorisation and commodification.

**PLAYERS** considers world-mending, and is structured by the guiding question: How can  indie video games help us reclaim our sociality from the threat of gamification?
![](players2.png)

**EMPIRES** considers commodification, and is structured by the guiding question: How can we imagine a future beyond the totalising power of Big Tech?
![](empires2.png)

**DATA POINTS** considers categorisation, and is structured by the guiding question: How can we protect our individuality from the mechanics of algorithmic governance?
![](datapoints2.png)

Miranda Mungai is an events producer, film curator and facilitator, taking particular interest in participatory methodologies and non-normative spaces for engaging with cinema, specialising in documentary and short form artists’ moving image. She worked as **Documentary Programmer at the London Short Film Festival** (2018 - 2022), where she was also **Industry Events Co-Producer** (2018 - 2021). Since then, she has worked as Festival Assistant at **Open City Documentary Festival** (2019), Talks Programme Assistant at **Sheffield Documentary Festival** (2021), Screening Days Advisory Group Member at the **Independent Cinema Office** (2023) and Communications Manager at **Berwick Film & Media Arts Festival** (2023 - 2026). She was selected for the King’s College London Undergraduate Research Fellowship in 2017 and as **Curatorial Fellow at the 68th Flaherty Film Seminar** in 2023.
